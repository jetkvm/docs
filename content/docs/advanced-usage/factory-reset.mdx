A factory reset reflashes JetKVM with the latest official firmware. Use it when you can't reach the device over SSH, want a clean slate, or are recovering from a failed boot.

## Pick your recovery method

There are two JetKVM hardware variants. Look at the right side of your device:

- If it has a removable **microSD** slot, use **microSD recovery**.
- If it has no card slot, use **DFU recovery** over USB.

The microSD slot is a small spring-loaded slot on the right side of the case. The device only boots from a card if it has the slot - if you don't see one, use DFU recovery.
